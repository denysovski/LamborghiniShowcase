Heritage & Legacy

History of Lamborghini

From a tractor manufacturer's passion project to a global symbol of innovation and luxury. Sixty years of relentless pursuit of performance.

1963

The Beginning

Ferruccio Lamborghini founded Automobili Lamborghini with a revolutionary vision: create the perfect grand touring car. The 350 GT debuts, forever changing automotive history.

350 GT • 3.9L V12 • 350 HP

Ferruccio’s original GT dream: elegant long-distance pace with a silk-smooth V12 and coachbuilt refinement.

350 GT • 3.9L V12 • 350 HP

Countach • 5.0L V12 • 455 HP

Countach • 5.0L V12 • 455 HP

The wedge revolution: scissor doors, outrageous angles, and poster-car presence that defined the supercar era.
1974

The Legend

The Countach arrives and sets the automotive world ablaze. Its wedge-shaped design and scissor doors define the supercar aesthetic for generations to come.

2001

Audi Era Begins

Audi Group acquisition marks a new chapter. The partnership brings technological advancement while preserving Lamborghini's distinctive soul and performance DNA.

Murciélago • 6.2L V12 • 580 HP

Precision meets passion: Audi-era engineering elevates build quality while keeping the raging bull’s V12 drama.

Murciélago • 6.2L V12 • 580 HP

Aventador • 6.5L V12 • 700 HP

Aventador • 6.5L V12 • 700 HP

Carbon monocoque mastery: lightning turn-in, wild theatrics, and the last pure-bred naturally aspirated V12 flagship.
2011

Modern Icon

The Aventador debuts with revolutionary carbon fiber monocoque construction and hybrid exhaust technology. A masterpiece of engineering and design.

2025

The Future is Now

The Revuelto arrives as the first HPEV Hybrid V12, marking the beginning of Lamborghini's electrified era. Performance and sustainability converge.

Revuelto • V12 Hybrid • 1015 HP

HPEV dawn: tri-motor hybrid torque with a roaring V12, ushering Lamborghini into electrified performance.

Revuelto • V12 Hybrid • 1015 HP
